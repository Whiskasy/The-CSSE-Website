The CSSE Chatbot

The answer to all your computer science and software engineering questions

What does it do?

CSSE Chatbot is a specialized AI chatbot that can answer all your computer science and software engineering questions. This includes frequently asked questions, general academic help and much more!

How does it work?

Simply type in your question in the chatbot window and the chatbot will provide you with an answer. It's that simple! Whenever you send a prompt, a call is made to the OpenRouter chatbot API, it uses the deepseek v3 model which processes the question and returns a CSSE related response. The chatbot is also capable of fetching details on GitHub repositories with the click of a button using the GitHub API. This allows you to get the latest information about a specific repository of your choice!

Why?

The CSSE Chatbot is a great tool to help students and professionals alike. It can help you with your assignments, projects, general queries or just help you spot plain oversights. It is a useful specialized tool for anyone who has questions about CSSE.
